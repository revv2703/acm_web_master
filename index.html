Who are WE?

ACM, Association for Computing Machinery is the world’s largest educational and scientific computing society, uniting educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources and address the field’s challenges. ACM bennett is a student chapter which fosters research and communication in a broad range of computing areas and focuses on solving tomorrow's problems.

How do we Work?!

How?!

ACM works to advance computing as a science and a profession.

It facilitates the exchange of ideas among members from all backgrounds and from all facets of computing, from academia to research to business and industry.

ACM serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.
